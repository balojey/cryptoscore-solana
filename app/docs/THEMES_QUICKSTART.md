## Choose Your Style!

CryptoScore now offers **6 beautiful themes** to customize your trading experience.

## How to Change Themes

### Method 1: Header Menu (Recommended)
1. Look for the theme button in the header (next to your wallet)
2. Click to open the theme menu
3. Select your preferred theme
4. See a live preview of colors before choosing

### Method 2: Keyboard Shortcut (Power Users)
- Press `Ctrl + Shift + T` (Windows/Linux)
- Press `Cmd + Shift + T` (Mac)
- Cycles through all themes instantly!
